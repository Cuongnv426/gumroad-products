Executive Summary Generator
Business
The Prompt
Create a concise executive summary for [DOCUMENT/PROJECT]. Include: key objectives, major findings, critical metrics, and recommended next steps. Format as a professional brief suitable for C-level presentation.
Use Cases
Sales decks, board reports, project pitches, quarterly reviews
Example
Input:
A 50-page market research report on AI adoption in retail
Output:
2-page executive summary with key metrics, trends, and recommendations
Pro Tips
  • Specify the target audience (CEO, investors, board) for better tone
  • Ask for specific metrics to include
  • Request it in PowerPoint format for faster implementation
How to Customize
Replace [DOCUMENT/PROJECT] with your specific content. Adjust length by saying '2-page' or '1-paragraph'

Email Sequence Copywriter
Marketing
The Prompt

Use Cases
Lead nurturing, product launches, re-engagement campaigns, webinar sign-ups
Example
Input:
5-email sequence for productivity app targeting solopreneurs struggling with time management
Output:
5 complete emails with subject lines, body copy, and conversion tactics
Pro Tips
  • Test variations of subject lines with A/B mention
  • Ask for segmentation strategies based on user behavior
  • Request mobile-optimized formatting
How to Customize
Adjust email count, goal, audience segment, and pain points. Add your brand voice details.
